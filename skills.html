KODIAK PROTEIN PANCAKES

Ingredients

  • Kodiak Protein Pancake Mix
  • Eggs
  • Almond Milk
  • Oikos Triple Zero Greek Yogurt
  • Vanilla Protein Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Blueberries (optional)

Directions

  1. Combine ingredients into a bowl and stir
  2. Heat pan and cover with Olive Oil
  3. Pour batter onto pan and drop blueberries
  4. Flip when bubbles appear and cook on other side
  5. Serve
